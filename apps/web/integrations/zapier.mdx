# How to Connect Smart Lead Magnet with Zapier

In this guide, we will explain how to use our native integration to add new leads and manage users through Zapier.

## 1. Go to Zapier.com

- **Log in** or **sign up** for a new account at [Zapier.com](https://zapier.com).
Zapier offers a free plan for up to 100 tasks per month, so you won't be charged.

## 2. Create a New Zap

- Click the **"Create Zap"** button.
- Choose a **Trigger** app. This could be any event that suits your workflow, such as a new subscription on Stripe, a successful payment on PayPal, or any other trigger.

## 3. Set Up the Trigger

- Follow the prompts to connect your trigger app and set up the trigger event.
- Test the trigger to ensure it's working correctly.

## 4. Add an Action to Add a New Lead

- In the **Action** step, search for **Smart Lead Magnet**.
- Select **Smart Lead Magnet** as the action app.
- Choose an action event, such as **Add New Lead** or **Create User**.
- Click **Continue** to configure the action.

## 5. Connect Your Smart Lead Magnet Account

- If you haven't connected your Smart Lead Magnet account before, you'll be prompted to do so.
- Follow the instructions to authorize Zapier to access your Smart Lead Magnet account.

## 6. Configure the Action

- You'll need to set up the following fields:

- **Email**: Select the email address from your trigger app data.
- **Name**: Select the name from your trigger app data.
- **Credits**: If applicable, specify the credit amount.
- **Portal**: Choose the relevant portal or project for the lead.

- Use the **Insert Data** button (the **"+"** icon) to map the data from the trigger event to these fields.

## 7. Test the Action

- Click **Test & Review** or **Test & Continue** to ensure everything is set up correctly.
- Check your Smart Lead Magnet account to verify that the new lead has been added.

## 8. Turn On Your Zap

- Once testing is successful, click **Turn on Zap** to activate it.
- Your Zap is now live and will automate adding new leads whenever the trigger event occurs.
